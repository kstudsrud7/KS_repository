Section 1

The membership of the Mashantucket (Western) Pequot Tribe shall consist of the following persons:

  • All persons whose names appear on either the “Twelfth Census of the United States (taken on June 15, 1900” or the “Thirteenth Census of the United States (taken on May 3, 1910)” and who are identified as Mashantucket (Western) Pequot, are herewith deemed and counted as “Full-Blood Mashantucket (Western) Pequot Indians” with the exception of Napoleon Langevin who was erroneously identified as a (Western) Pequot Indian.
  • All persons who are recognized and enrolled as Members of the Mashantucket (Western) Pequot Tribe as of November 3, 1996.
Section 2

Direct lineal descendants of tribal members who are recognized as members of the Mashantucket (Western) Pequot Tribe as of November 3, 1996, shall be eligible for membership.

Section 3

No person shall be considered a member of the Mashantucket (Western) Pequot Tribe while his/her name is on the official roll of any other tribe of American Indians.

Required Documents for Higher Education Assistance Program

To complete your application, please ensure you have the following documents ready for upload. Missing documents may delay processing.

Document Checklist:
  • Acceptance Letter: Please upload an official acceptance letter from your educational institution. This document should clearly indicate your admission status and the program you have been accepted into.

Required Verification Documentation

To proceed with your application, you must provide the necessary verification documentation. Missing or incomplete documents may result in delays in processing your request.

Document Details:
  • Verification Document: If tuition & fees statement is available, please upload below. The documents can include:
    • A detailed breakdown of your tuition and fees.
    • Your name and the institution's name.
    • The term or semester the invoice covers.
    This document is required to verify the costs associated with your education program.

Required Verification Documentation

To proceed with your application, you must submit the necessary verification documentation. Missing or incomplete documents may result in delays in processing your request.

Document Details:
  • Institution’s W9: Please upload a completed W9 form from your educational institution. The document should include:
    • Class schedule for current term (with name and/or student ID visible, with the corresponding number of units, and start and end dates for each class)
    • Current Academic Transcript
    • Education plans (developed with the Educational Institution, see Program Rules for Education Plan required elements)
    This document is required for verification of the institution’s tax information.
