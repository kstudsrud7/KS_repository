Application for Membership

Into the Mashantucket (Western) Pequot Tribe

Article IV of the Constitution of the Mashantucket (Western) Pequot Tribe provides the following definition for persons eligible for membership into the tribe:

Section 1. The membership of the Mashantucket (Western) Pequot Tribe shall consist of the following persons:

  • All persons whose names appear on either the “Twelfth Census of the United States (taken on June 15, 1900” or the “Thirteenth Census of the United States (taken on May 3, 1910)” and who are identified as Mashantucket (Western) Pequot, are herewith deemed and counted as “Full-Blood Mashantucket (Western) Pequot Indians” with the exception of Napoleon Langevin who was erroneously identified as a (Western) Pequot Indian.
  • All persons who are recognized and enrolled as Members of the Mashantucket (Western) Pequot Tribe as of November 3, 1996.

Section 2. Direct lineal descendants of tribal members who are recognized as members of the Mashantucket (Western) Pequot Tribe as of November 3, 1996, shall be eligible for membership.

Section 3. No person shall be considered a member of the Mashantucket (Western) Pequot Tribe while his/her name is on the official roll of any other tribe of American Indians.

Any individual who meets these criteria may complete the following application.

Important – Please Read the Following:

Required Documentation

  1. Certified copy of birth certificate bearing official seal, identifying parentage and evidencing lineal descendancy to a tribal member on the 1900, 1910, and November 3, 1996 base roll of the Mashantucket (Western) Pequot Tribe.
  2. DNA testing shall be required for the applicant and the applicant’s tribal parent as necessary to establish direct lineal descendancy. (Any cost to show the burden of proof of direct lineal descendancy is the responsibility of the applicant.)
  3. If name change has occurred, marriage certificate or court record and any other documentation concerning the change in name.
  4. Social Security card (for copy).
  5. If applicant is legally adopted we must have original birth certificate with tribal parent listed. If not available then please explain why in a letter addressed to the Elder’s Council.

B. This application will not be reviewed until it is completed and all required documentation provided. (Please note, applications with cross outs or other edits will not be accepted)

C. If more than one person is applying for membership, each person must fill out a separate application. Tribal member parent or guardian may fill out an application if applicant is under 18 years old or unable to fill out the application.
