Welcome to the Tribal Community Engagement Portal. The mission of the Tribal Government is to provide services to members anytime, anyplace, on any device. This member portal is designed to help you securely engage with staff, update your member information, and request services and information at your convenience.

Ways you can use this portal:

Submit an Enrollment Application

View details and submit enrollment applications to tribal staff.

Update your Medical Records

Provide details on your medical provider(s), health insurance, and more.

Update your Household

View and edit the members of your household. Used for application eligibility.

Address Change Request

View history and submit a change in your physical address.

Membership Change Request

Submit a request to change your Tribe membership status.

ID Card Request

Submit a request to (re)print a Tribal Identification Card.

Make a change request

As an Enrollment user, you can discover your program eligibility and submit change requests including requesting a new ID card, relinquish membership, add a relationship, and more.
