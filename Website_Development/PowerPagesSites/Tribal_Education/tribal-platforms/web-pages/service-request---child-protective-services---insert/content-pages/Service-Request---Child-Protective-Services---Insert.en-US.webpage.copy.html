Protective Services

The Child Protection Services program conducts child protection investigations on all neglect and abuse complaints within the Tribe’s jurisdiction.

The Child Protection Case Management program provides ongoing child welfare case management and prevention services to Tribe members, first-generation descendants, and American Indians within the Tribe’s designated service area.

The Indian Child Welfare program monitors the welfare of all Tribe children and first-generation descendants outside the Tribe’s designated service area under the Indian Child Welfare Act.
