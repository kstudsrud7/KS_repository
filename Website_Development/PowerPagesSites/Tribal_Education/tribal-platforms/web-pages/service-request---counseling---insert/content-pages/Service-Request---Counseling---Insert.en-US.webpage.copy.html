Counseling Services

Mental health diagnostic assessments and counseling are provided to individuals and families. Some of the programing and counseling services include: Batterers’ Intervention, Individual Therapy for adults, Individual Therapy for children and adolescents, Trauma Informed Therapy, Play Therapy, and Mental Health Case Management.
