Service Request Options 


The Tribal Government Family Services Department provides social services to members and American Indians who reside in the identified service area of the Tribe. The Family Services Department offers several services to help you and your family. Important service areas are noted below and will take you to the application to submit a service request.

My Open Service Requests

View a list of service requests I have submitted to the Family Service staff.

Request Financial Assistance

Assist families and individuals through a difficult financial situation.

Counseling Request

One on one assistance to developing independent life skills.

Treatment Request

Individual and family support for substance abuse victims to return to a healthy lifestyle.

Child or Adult Protective Services

Individualized crisis support for vulnerable children or adults.

General Request

General family and individual support and services.

24/7 secure access to your information

With the Community Engagement Portal, you can ensure your data remains private and confidential. Only authorized Tribal Government staff members have access to the information shared through this site.
