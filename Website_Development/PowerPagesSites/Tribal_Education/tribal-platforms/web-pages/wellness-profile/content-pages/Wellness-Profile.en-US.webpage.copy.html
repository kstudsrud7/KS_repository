Wellness Recommendations


The Tribal Government Family Wellness Department provides services to members and American Indians who reside in the identified service area of the Tribe. The Family Wellness Department offers several services to help you and your family. Important wellness areas are noted below and will take you to a corresponding web page.

Driver License Information

Submit changes to update current drivers license information.

Medical Information

Update medical and insurance information to your profile. Add medical history to your account along with a preferred pharmacy.

Outcomes

Check the list of outcomes that are related to your contact profile. 

Goals

View a list of goals added by the caseworker associated to your account.

Cases

Find cases added to your profile by your caseworker.

24/7 secure access to your information

With the Community Engagement Portal, you can ensure your data remains private and confidential. Only authorized Tribal Government staff members have access to the information shared through this site.
