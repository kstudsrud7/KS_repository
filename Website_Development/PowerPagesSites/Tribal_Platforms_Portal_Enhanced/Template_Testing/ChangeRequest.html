Change Requests

Use this page to submit and track requests for updates to your information. Common requests include updating your contact information, changing relationships, or requesting a new ID card.

Examples of Change Requests:
  • Updating your email address or phone number.
  • Adding or updating an emergency contact.
  • Changing household relationships (e.g., adding a new family member).
  • Requesting a new or replacement ID card.
  • Providing additional documentation for a pending request.
